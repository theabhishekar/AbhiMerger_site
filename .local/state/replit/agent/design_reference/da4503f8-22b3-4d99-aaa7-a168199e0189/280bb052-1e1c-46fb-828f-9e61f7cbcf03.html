5-Step Workflow

Simple, intuitive process that gets you from Excel to merged PDF in minutes

1

Upload Excel File

Simply drag and drop your Excel file (.xlsx or .xls) into AbhiMerger. The app instantly analyzes your spreadsheet and identifies potential PDF references.

2

Browse Interactive Table

View your Excel data in a clean, searchable table. Use powerful filtering and search capabilities to quickly locate the PDF references you need.

3

Select PDF Paths

Click on cells containing PDF paths to select them. Bulk selection via checkboxes for rows and columns makes it easy to handle large datasets.

4

Validate & Merge

AbhiMerger validates all PDF paths and begins the merge process. Real-time progress tracking keeps you informed every step of the way.

5

Preview & Save

Preview your merged PDF with the built-in viewer, then save it to your desired location. Professional results every time with comprehensive error handling.
